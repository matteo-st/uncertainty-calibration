\textbf{Combined Reliability Diagram (Doctor Score).}
Comparison of three calibration approaches on ImageNet with ViT-Base16 (50 bins).
\textbf{Blue circles}: Conservatively calibrated using upper bound $\hat{u}(b)$; all points lie below the diagonal ($y=x$), demonstrating the guarantee from Corollary~4.1 with 0/50 violations.
\textbf{Green diamonds}: Calibrated using mean $\hat{p}_{\mathrm{cal}}(b)$; points cluster around the diagonal (ECE=0.0102) with 25/50 violations, showing standard calibration without conservative guarantees.
\textbf{Orange triangles}: Doctor score $\bar{s}(b)$; points deviate substantially from the diagonal (ECE=0.0539) with 44/50 violations, demonstrating that Doctor is not calibrated as an error probability estimator.
The Y-axis shows the empirical test error rate for all methods.
